Welcome to the Classical Sketchbook

The Classical Sketchbook celebrates the sketches, cartoons, and drawings of classical artists from Masaccio to Michelangelo to Ingres. Tour the gallery images to learn more about these beautiful and subtle works of art.

Like what you see? Visit our Online shop for quality merchandise. We sell t-shirts, coffee mugs, posters, many other items with beautifully-reproduced images from the old masters.

If you're interested in learning more about the history and stories behind these works of art, visit our large library of articles and lectures delivered by noted experts in the field. We are adding more material every month, so check back often!
